# Project 3: Demand Estimation and Market Analysis — Air Fryers

**Course:** DS 3001 — Foundations of ML  
**Group members:** Simon Alam, Miky Asmare, Isaiah Lucero, Andrew Patterson

## Overview
Demand estimation analysis of the air fryer market using brand-year data from Amazon purchases (2019–2023). We fit a multinomial logit demand model to estimate price sensitivity, infer unit costs and markups, and identify which brands hold the strongest market positions.

## Repository Structure
- `notebooks/notebook_1_data_analyst.ipynb` — EDA: prices, ratings, and market shares over time
- `notebooks/notebook_2_data_scientist.ipynb` — Demand estimation via OLS on log market share
- `notebooks/notebook_3_pricing_analyst.ipynb` — Unit costs, markups, and share-weighted profits
- `air_fryer_market_intelligence.pptx` — Business presentation of findings
